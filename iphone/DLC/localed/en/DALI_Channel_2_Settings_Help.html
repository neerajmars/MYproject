DALI Channel 2 Settings Help

DALI Channel 2 can be configured to behave in a number of ways from this page.

Features: (Touch for more information)

Switch Inputs

You can modify the functionality of the switch inputs for DALI channel 1 (inputs 5 and 6 on the manual switch interface). This enables the use of different retractive (momentary) switch types: Single-Pole Switch = a simple push to make switch. Double-Pole Switch = a centre-biased switch with an up and a down position. For more information, please refer to the Installation Guide.

Output Settings

Manual Control Only = The channel will only respond to manual switch control and will not be controlled by the PIR and Timers. PIR/Timer/Manual = Responds to presence detection from the PIR, will turn off automatically when no presence is detected and can be manually controlled. Mimic Channel 1 = Makes the channel follow channel 1 with a controllable offset. Use this if constant light is required on DALI channel 2. Manual switch inputs for channel 2 are redirected to channel 1 in this mode.

DALI 2 Reset

This can be used to reset settings in connected DALI load devices to optimise their control performance on iDim Orbit.
