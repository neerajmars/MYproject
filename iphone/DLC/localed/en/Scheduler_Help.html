Scheduler Help

This page allows the scheduler to be enabled and disabled and for the start time of Profiles 1 and 2 to be edited for each day of the week.

Features: (Touch for more information)

Enable/Disable

Enable the scheduler to start editing the start times.

Schedule Editor

Shows the start time for Profile 1 and 2 for each day of the week. Touch a particular time entry to open the editor. Use the scroll wheels to edit the time and press Save to store the value into the scheduler. Either continue selecting and editing individual entries by touching on the display or scroll through the day of the week and Profiles in the editor. Remember to press save to store each entry. Press Sync to upload the settings to iDim Orbit. Touch outside of the editor on a grey area to close the editor.
