DALI Channel 1 Settings Help

DALI Channel 1 is always connected to the PIR, Timers and manual switch inputs. When constant light is enabled, DALI channel 1 will be controlled automatically to meet the target level.

Features: (Touch for more information)

Switch Inputs

You can modify the functionality of the switch inputs for DALI channel 1 (inputs 2 and 3 on the manual switch interface). This enables the use of different retractive (momentary) switch types: Single-Pole Switch A simple push to make switch. Double-Pole Switch = a centre-biased switch with an up and a down position. For more information, please refer to the Installation Guide.

DALI 1 Reset

This can be used to reset settings in connected DALI load devices to optimise their control performance on iDim Orbit.
